Where Beauty Meets Elegance

ADHI Salon was founded with a vision to create a sanctuary where clients could experience transformative beauty treatments in a luxurious, welcoming environment. Our journey began in a small studio and has grown into Colombo's premier beauty destination.

We combine cutting-edge techniques with time-honored traditions, using only the finest products from around the world. Our team of internationally trained stylists and therapists are dedicated to helping you look and feel your absolute best.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our salon and services.

While we do accept walk-ins, we highly recommend making an appointment to ensure availability with your preferred stylist and to minimize waiting time. You can book online through our website, via WhatsApp, or by calling the salon directly.

Your safety is our top priority. We follow strict hygiene protocols including:

  • Regular sanitization of all tools and surfaces
  • Use of disposable items where possible
  • Staff wearing masks and gloves during treatments
  • Temperature checks for all clients and staff
  • Limited capacity to ensure social distancing

We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Appointments canceled with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a cancellation fee of 50% of the service cost. No-shows will be charged the full amount of the scheduled service.

Yes! We offer comprehensive bridal packages that include trial sessions, bridal makeup, hairstyling, and optional nail services. We also have special occasion packages for events like proms, engagements, and photoshoots. Contact us for a consultation to customize a package that fits your needs.

We use only premium professional-grade products from trusted brands like Olaplex, Kerastase, Wella, OPI, and Dermalogica. All our products are cruelty-free and many are vegan. Our stylists can recommend products for at-home maintenance that complement your salon treatments.
